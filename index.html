Styles of Dance

Bharatanatyam (Tamil Nadu)

Bharatanatyam is a classical Indian dance form that combines intricate footwork, expressive gestures, and rhythmic patterns to tell stories. Originating from Tamil Nadu, it is a beautiful blend of spirituality, grace, and tradition.

kathakali (Kerala)

Kathakali is a traditional dance-drama form from Kerala, characterized by vibrant costumes, elaborate makeup, and powerful facial expressions. It beautifully combines storytelling, music, and dance to portray ancient mythological tales with intense emotion.

Read More
Kuchipudi (Andhra Pradesh)

Kuchipudi is a classical Indian dance form from Andhra Pradesh, known for its graceful movements, intricate footwork, and expressive storytelling. It blends dance, drama, and music to depict mythological themes with elegance.

Read More
kathak (North India)

Kathak is a classical Indian dance form that originated in North India, known for its intricate footwork, spins, and expressive storytelling. It beautifully blends grace, rhythm, and storytelling to narrate mythological and cultural tales with elegance.

Read More
Bhangra & Gidda (Punjab)

Bhangra is a lively and energetic folk dance from Punjab, characterized by vigorous footwork, rhythmic drumming, and vibrant music. Gidda, the female counterpart, showcases graceful, lively movements and joyful expressions, celebrating the rich cultural heritage of Punjab.

Read More
Garba & Dandiya Raas (Gujarat)

Garba is a traditional Gujarati dance performed in a circle, featuring rhythmic clapping and graceful movements, often during Navratri. Dandiya Raas involves couples dancing with colorful sticks, creating a lively, synchronized display of rhythm and tradition.

Read More
Bihu (Assam)

Bihu is a traditional folk dance form from Assam, characterized by lively, rhythmic movements and colorful attire. It celebrates the Assamese New Year and harvest season, showcasing vibrant energy, joy and cultural expression.

Read More
Yakshagana (Karnataka)

Yakshagana is a traditional Indian dance-drama form from Karnataka, combining storytelling, music, and vibrant dance. It is known for its elaborate costumes, makeup, and expressive movements, depicting mythological and historical themes.

Read More
Jazz

Jazz dance is a dynamic and expressive dance style that blends energetic movements, improvisation, and rhythm. Rooted in African-American culture, it incorporates elements from ballet, modern dance, and popular music, offering versatility and flair.

Read More
Hip Hop

Hip-hop is a dynamic and energetic dance style that originated in the streets, characterized by freestyle movements, locking, popping, and breaking. It is often performed to hip-hop music and focuses on rhythm, individuality, and self-expression.

Read More
Salsa

Salsa is a lively and rhythmic dance style that originated in Latin America, characterized by energetic footwork, spins, and partner coordination. It blends Afro-Cuban, jazz, and other Latin influences, creating a passionate and vibrant social dance.

Read More
Freestyle

Freestyle dance is an improvised, spontaneous dance style where dancers express themselves freely to the music, showcasing creativity, emotion, and unique personal style. It emphasizes fluid movements, rhythm, energy and individuality.
